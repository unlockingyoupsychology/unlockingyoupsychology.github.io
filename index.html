Meet your Clinical Psychologist

Jules Mascarenhas

Jules is a clinical psychologist and the founder of Unlocking You Psychology. She has extensive experience working with people of all ages experiencing a range of mental health challenges. She uses a trauma-informed and holistic approach to therapy, recognizing that true mental wellness comes from integrating the mind, body and emotions.

She works with you to better understand deeper layers of yourself and move toward the changes you want to make—whether they’re short-term goals or part of longer-term personal growth.

  • Accredited APHRA service provider

  • 9+ years of clinical experience

  • Verified by Psychology Today
