Meet your Clinical Psychologist

Jules Mascarenhas

Jules founded Unlocking You Psychology with a mission to support people of all ages in navigating and improving their mental health

She uses a trauma-informed and holistic approach to therapy, recognizing that true mental wellness comes from integrating the mind, body and emotions.

She works with you to better understand deeper layers of yourself and move toward the changes you want to make—whether they're short-term goals or part of longer-term personal growth.
