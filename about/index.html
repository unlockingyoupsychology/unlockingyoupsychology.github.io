Meet Your Clinical Psychologist

Jules is a clinical psychologist and the founder of Unlocking You Psychology. She has extensive experience working with adults and young people affected by complex trauma and mental health disorders in various settings.

She is passionate about creating a safe, dynamic space for growth and healing. With a trauma-informed, holistic approach, she supports long-term personal transformation that integrates mind, body, and emotion.

Jules welcomes all people and is dedicated to fostering trust, connection, and understanding in her therapeutic work.
